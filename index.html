Daniel Won

Front End Developer

Hi, I'm Dan.

I’m a front-end developer based in Chicago with a background in classical music and nonprofit management. During the pandemic, I discovered my passion for web development and have spent the past 12 months honing my programming skills and learning new technology. Coding practically everyday for a year, and loving every second of it, made me realize that this is what I want to do—so I’ve decided to take the leap to pursue a career in tech.

As a professional clarinetist, I have performed with world-class ensembles, including the Chicago Symphony Orchestra and the Joffrey Ballet. Being able to perform at the highest level, under immense pressure, has equipped me with qualities and skills that are essential for a successful software developer: ability to analyze complex problems and solve them methodically within a timeframe;  self-discipline, drive, and willpower to achieve goals—and patience along the way;  desire to constantly improve;  and being able to work independently as well as with others . I am confident that these qualities, along with incredible correlation between music and coding, will help me become a strong developer.

It has been an incredible journey so far, and I am extremely excited about the idea of building amazing products to help people. If you’d like to learn more about me, please don’t hesitate to get in touch!
